Band description

Tabula Rasa is a band formed in 2023 exploring new areas of rock, creating a unique combination of experimental rock, electronic and hard-rock. Their music is a fusion of powerful, greasy guitar riffs with modern synthesizers that give the songs a mesmerizing, futuristic character. It's music full of contrasts and experiments - from strong, hard rock beats through funk rock and post grunge to subtle, nostalgic sound spaces.

The band consists of musicians:

  • Beniamin Skoniecki - vocals and lead guitar
  • Delia Janiszewska - keyboards
  • Johnny Mitrowski - rhythm guitar
  • Carlos Ceron Lara - bass guitar
  • Bartosz Kamiński - drums

Since the beginning of its activity, Tabula Rasa has performed at numerous prestigious events, gaining recognition from both fans and music critics.
