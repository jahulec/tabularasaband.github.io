Band biography

Tabula Rasa is a dynamic rock band from Warsaw, which, since its creation in 2023, has been gaining more and more popularity on the Polish music scene. The band's name, referring to the concept of a "blank slate", perfectly reflects the spirit of creativity, experimentation and unlimited potential that lies in each member of the group. The band consists of talented musicians:

  • Beniamin Skoniecki - vocals and lead guitar
  • Delia Janiszewska - keyboards
  • Johnny Mitrowski - rhythm guitar
  • Carlos Ceron Lara - bass guitar
  • Bartosz Kamiński - drums

Since the beginning of its activity, Tabula Rasa has performed at numerous prestigious events, gaining recognition from both fans and music critics.

Achievements

  • 2023: Stodoła - First place in the Warsaw University of Technology contest.
  • 2023: PW Juwenalia together with Waluś, Kraksa, Kryzys, Zalewski and Kult.
  • 2023: Żywiec - collaboration and performance at “The Smallest Stage in the World”.
  • 2023: Rock Radio - Audience Award in the Classic Rock Competition.
  • 2023: Akademia Rock Festival - performance as a special guest.
  • 2024: WOŚP - 2 charity concerts in Warsaw.
  • 2024: Rock May Festival - Audience Award.
  • 2024: Knurowisko - Second place.
  • 2024: Wieli Ogień in honor of Mira Kubasińska - First place.
  • 2024: Śmigasówka Festival.
  • 2024: Teleexpress - promotion of the new single “Everything That I Have”.
  • 2024: Rock And Love Festival.
  • 2024: TVP Kielce - promotion of the new single “Last Time”.
  • 2024: Invitation to the Akademia Rock Festival as the star of the evening.
  • 2025: WOŚP - charity concert on the big stage in Będzin.
  • 2025: New single “OFFLINE”.
