Band Description

Tabula Rasa is a Warsaw-based rock band formed in 2023. The group blends experimental rock, electronic textures, and hard rock into a sound built on heavy guitar riffs and modern synth layers.

The songs move between high-energy hooks and darker, atmospheric passages, combining funk-rock drive, post-grunge weight, and contemporary production.

Lineup

  • Beniamin Skoniecki - vocals and guitar
  • Delia Janiszewska - keyboards
  • Johnny Mitrowski - lead guitar
  • Carlos Ceron Lara - bass
  • Bartosz Kamiński - drums

Since its debut, Tabula Rasa has performed at major events and festivals, building strong momentum with both audiences and music media.
