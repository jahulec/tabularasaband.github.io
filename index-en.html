News

We won the Road To Juwe contest in Płock!

At the beginning of May, we took part in the Road to Juwe contest in Płock, where we won a performance at the Płock Juwenalia on May 22! This is already our 3rd juwenalia this year! Big congratulations to rapper Kayak for co-winning this showcase!

We're playing at the Bydgoszcz Juwenalia!

We have another performance at juwenalia, this time in Bydgoszcz! We’ll be sharing the stage with legends like Dżem and Lej mi Pół. You can check all juwenalia gigs in the concerts tab.

We won the Kill Me With Music contest in Rzeszów!

In April, we took part in the Kill Me With Music contest in Rzeszów and won a performance at the Rzeszów Juwenalia! We’re incredibly thrilled with this recognition! We'll play on May 9 on the biggest stage alongside Lady Pank, Ich Troje, and Pidżama Porno!

New track “Hybryda” out now!

We’ve released our new single “Hybryda”, which tells the story of breaking away from toxic people and finding peace in solitude — a time to focus on yourself. The music video was filmed at the best rock school — Akademia Rocka in Warsaw (Targowa), portraying a futuristic version of a human — the Hybrid.

We took 2nd place at PKS Lublin 2025!

In March, we took part in the Student Bands Review in Lublin, where we performed among other high-level bands. We managed to secure 2nd place, which we’re really proud of — especially since we played this show without Delia ;) Congrats to the winners — Kwazzy Live Band!

New track “Offline” out now!

We’ve officially released another single, “Offline”. The song is about disconnecting from media and electronics. It presents a world where everyone is glued to their phones. The main character encourages us to break free and shows what the world looks like when we do. The music video was shot in the Warsaw metro.

Tabula Rasa in the Finals of the IGRY 2025 Festival!

In January, we had the incredible pleasure of performing at the Spirala student club in Silesia, where we took part in the IGRY 2025 festival qualifiers. Together with the band Viadomo, we managed to advance to the finals, which is a huge honor for us. Now, the next stage awaits us – the grand final of the qualifiers, which will determine who gets to perform at the IGRY 2025 Festival!
