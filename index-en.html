Tabula Rasa in the Finals of the IGRY 2025 Festival!

The new year started in the best possible way for us! In January, we had the incredible pleasure of performing at the **Spirala** student club in Silesia, where we took part in the **IGRY 2025** festival qualifiers. The competition was fierce – four bands took the stage, each giving their all to secure a place in the finals.

Together with the band Viadomo, we managed to advance to the finals, which is a huge honor for us.

We want to thank everyone who was there with us – the organizers, the audience, and the other bands that created an amazing atmosphere that night. Now, the next stage awaits us – the **grand final of the qualifiers**, which will determine who gets to perform at the **IGRY 2025 Student Festival**! We already know it's going to be an unforgettable battle of sound and emotions.

Keep your fingers crossed for us and follow our social media – big things are coming!
