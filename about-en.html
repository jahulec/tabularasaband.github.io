Who we are

Tabula Rasa is a dynamic rock band from Warsaw, quickly gaining momentum on the Polish music scene. The name, inspired by the idea of a "blank slate," reflects our openness to experimentation, contrast, and new sonic directions.

Sound

Our music combines melodic hooks and heavy riffs, blending emotional songwriting with high-energy performance. Each song is built to carry both weight and atmosphere.

Live presence

Since 2023, we have played major events and festivals, earning recognition from audiences, organizers, and music media.

Tabula Rasa is more than a band name. It is an evolving project driven by passion, ambition, and a clear artistic identity.
