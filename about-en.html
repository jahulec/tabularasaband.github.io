Who are we?

Tabula Rasa is a dynamic rock band from Warsaw that is quickly gaining recognition on the Polish music scene. The band's name, inspired by the concept of a "blank slate", symbolizes unlimited potential, creativity and the courage to experiment. Since our founding in 2023, our music has attracted the attention of fans and critics, and our performances at prestigious events and festivals confirm our growing popularity. Our sound is a unique mixture of melodic sounds and strong riffs that create a harmonious whole, taking listeners on an emotional journey. Each of our songs is a combination of deep lyrics and expressive performance that touches hearts and remains in memory long after the concert ends. Tabula Rasa is not just music - it is an experience that moves and inspires. We have won prestigious competitions and cooperated with renowned brands. We play on the largest stages, from clubs to festivals, always providing unforgettable emotions. We are a band that is constantly developing, and our concerts are events full of passion and energy. We invite you to discover our music and join our rock journey. Tabula Rasa is a band that exceeds the boundaries of convention, and its potential is only just beginning to be realized.
