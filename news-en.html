Grand Prix at Wszyscy Spiewamy na Rockowo

We came home from the final with the Grand Prix. More than a trophy, it is a strong sign that our new material and live energy are reaching the right place.

Some evenings stay with you. You step onto the stage, play your music, and a few moments later learn that the jury has given you the competition's highest award.

Thank you to the jury, the event crew, and everyone who stood with us in front of the stage. This win does not close the story. It travels with us to the next cities and the next shows.

March 2, 2026Live

Progresja and a night with Smith/Kotzen

A big room, a focused crowd, and that particular moment just before the first note. This is exactly why we play live.

We stepped onto the stage at Warsaw's Progresja as support for Smith/Kotzen. From the first minutes, we felt the room's attention and the kind of tension that turns a show into a real meeting.

That evening stays with us. We will return to it in photos and backstage material, but what mattered most was the moment shared with the audience right there and then.
